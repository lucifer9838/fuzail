Academics Navigator

‘It is better to take small steps in the right direction than to make a great leap forward only to stumble backward.’ – This proverb describes the basic idea of Academics Navigator. Career planning is a significant part of our lives today. Even when one is aware about their goals, choosing the right career path could be an overwhelming task for majority of the students and that is where our application steps in.
With ‘Academics Navigator’, we aim to guide the users towards achieving their goals step by step. Also, it involves bringing people together who share common goals which will help them receive accountability and gain clarity.
